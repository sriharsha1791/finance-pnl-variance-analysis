# Budget vs Actual vs Forecast

## Budget

Meaning:

Planned target before year starts.

Examples:

Revenue Budget = 120 Cr

Marketing Budget = 20 Cr

Hiring Plan = 50 Employees

Budget answers:

"What do we expect?"

---

## Actual

Meaning:

Real business performance.

Example:

Budget Revenue = 120 Cr

Actual Revenue = 110 Cr

Actual answers:

"What really happened?"

---

## Forecast

Meaning:

Updated expectation after seeing actual performance.

Example:

Original Budget Revenue = 120 Cr

After weak January:

Forecast Revenue = 115 Cr

Forecast answers:

"What do we think will happen now?"

---

## Variance

Formula:

Variance = Actual - Budget

Example:

Budget Revenue = 100 Cr

Actual Revenue = 90 Cr

Variance = -10 Cr

Negative revenue variance means target missed.
